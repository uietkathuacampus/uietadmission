Admission Policy

Eligibility Criteria:

Candidate should have Passed Hr. Sec. Part II (10+2) or equivalent with 50% (45% for SC/ST) in PCM from J&K Board of School education or any recognized board with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics.


Selection Criteria:

Admission to the B. Tech. Programmes for the Academic Session 2025-26 shall be made on the basis of following preferences:-

  1. JEE Main score of 2025.
  2. CUET-(UG)-25 composite score in domain subjects of Physics/Computer science and Mathematics
  3. If seats remain vacant, University of Jammu shall conduct a test to admit the candidates against the remaining vacant seats.

Note:-

  1. General Jammu (70%)
    1. Candidate holding the domicile of Jammu Division but studied anywhere.
    2. Candidate studied in the recognized schools within the Jurisdiction of Jammu Division.
  2. General Outside (30%)
    1. Candidate outside of Jammu Division and having not less than 60% marks in 10+2.
